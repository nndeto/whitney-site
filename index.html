About

Through data-driven analysis, I develop interactivity, processes, or any training content to bridge the gap between the lowest and highest performers. I help organizations find solutions, implement change, and foster growth.

Organizations who have used my services found actual solutions not ‘quick wins,’ improved efficiency in employees doing their job, mitigated risk, and lowered the cost of poor performers executing errors.

But who am I?

I'm just a 30 something, Latina who loves food and travel. I have 5+ years of instructional design experience building R.O.I., and I develop deep empathy with the people I'm designing for...keeping the learner in the center.
